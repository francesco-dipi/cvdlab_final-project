Cigar Floor Lamp

Measurements:
Small: H 54" Diameter 10" Cord L 72"
Medium: H 59" Diameter 11.5" Cord L 72"
Large: H 63.5" Diameter 14" Cord L 72"

George Nelson's Cigar Lamp(1947) is one of the most popular lamps in the George Nelson Bubble Lamp collection and a Stardust Staff Pick. Part of the permanent collection of New York's Museum of Modern Art, these stylish modern cigar-shaped light fixtures have stood the test of time. They are constructed of a steel wire frame and covered with translucent plastic to emit a soft, even light. The George Nelson Cigar Bubble Lamp is a beloved icon of mid-century modern design and sets the right mood in any interior space. George Nelson designed the Bubble Lamp collection for Howard Miller in 1947, the Cigar Lamp is available in three sizes.
“When you put a light in it, it glowed.”

Modeling

The model of the Cigar Lamp is composed by three parts:
The lamp base is the structure of the lamp and has been created using the function ROTATIONAL_SOLID starting from the profile points.

The shade is generated rotating a curve using the ROTATIONAL_SURFACE function.

The shade structure is generated duplicating and rotating a NUBSPLINE with the function DUPLIROT.
